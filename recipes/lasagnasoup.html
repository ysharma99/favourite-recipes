Lasagna Soup

The Yummiest Lasagna Soup Ever!

The lasagna vibes in one simple, comforting bowl — with just a few ingredients!

What you will need!

  • 250g ground chicken
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
  • 3 cups chicken or veggie broth
  • 5–6 lasagna sheets (broken into pieces)
  • Grated cheese (mozzarella or whatever you like)

Instructions

  1. Cook meat, onion & garlic in a pot.
  2. Add tomatoes & broth, bring to a boil.
  3. Add pasta, simmer 10–12 mins.
  4. Season & top with cheese. Done!
