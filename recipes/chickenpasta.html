The Best Creamy Chicken Pasta Recipe

This Creamy Chicken Pasta Recipe is a comforting and easy meal perfect for busy weeknights. With tender chicken, garlic, herbs, and a rich parmesan sauce, it’s a crowd-pleaser that comes together in just 30 minutes. You can customise it with veggies or keep it simple—either way, it’s delicious!

What you will need:

  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced thin)
  • 250g pasta (penne, fettuccine, or your fave)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 small onion (diced)
  • 1 cup cream (or evaporated milk for a lighter version)
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs (or mixed dried herbs)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Optional: spinach, mushrooms, or cherry tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to the packet. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook chicken: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and herbs. Cook for 4–5 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove from pan.
  3. in the same pan for 2–3 minutes until soft. (Add mushrooms or tomatoes here if using.)
  4. Add cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in parmesan and cook until the sauce thickens (about 3–5 minutes).
  5. Combine: Add cooked pasta and chicken back into the pan. Toss to coat. Stir in spinach at the end if using.
  6. Serve hot, topped with extra parmesan and black pepper.
