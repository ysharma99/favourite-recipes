Honey Soy Chicken Stir-Fry

The best stirfry you will try!

Stir fry is a fast and versatilecooking method where bite-sized pieces of meat and vegetables are cooked quickly over high heat. It’s a great way to make a healthy, flavour-packed meal using simple ingredients and sauces. Perfect for busy days, stir fry is ready in minutes and can be customised with your favourite veggies, proteins, and seasonings.

What you will need!

  • 1 chicken breast (sliced thin)
  • 1–2 cups mixed veggies (like capsicum, broccoli, carrot)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil or honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Cook chicken until browned.
  3. Add garlic and veggies, stir-fry 3–5 mins.
  4. Add soy sauce (and sesame oil/honey if using).
  5. Stir well and cook for another 2 mins. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
